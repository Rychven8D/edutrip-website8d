Welcome to My Edutrip Experience in Yogyakarta!

During this amazing educational journey, I had the opportunity to stay with a local family as part of a Live In experience. It gave me a chance to live like a local, learn their daily routines, traditions, and values.

I visited many fascinating places including museums, temples, monuments, and other cultural attractions. From learning about ancient history at Museum Sangiran, to admiring the beautiful architecture of Candi Prambanan, this trip was filled with educational adventures.

Each subject has its own special task related to the places we visited. Feel free to explore the pages above to see what I learned in each subject!
