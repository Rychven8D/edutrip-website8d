English Assignment

  1. Read the itinerary of your Edutrip
  2. Decide what topic you want to write about for your travel writing article (food/place/experience)
  3. Create a mindmap on a piece of paper. Write the topic in the middle and ask at least 10 WH questions to answer in your article
  4. Take a photo of your mindmap and upload it to Google Classroom
  5. Paste the mindmap on your Edutrip workbook
